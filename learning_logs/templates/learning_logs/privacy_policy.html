Privacy Policy for Django-Chronics Social Media Website

1. Data Collection:

When users choose to log in using Google or Facebook authentication, Django-Chronics will collect the following information: For Google Auth: Name, Email, For Facebook Auth: Name,

2. Data Usage:

The collected data is used for account creation and display purposes of the user's profile. Username or emails may be used as account names.

3. User Control:

Users can edit or delete their accounts directly from their account page on Django-Chronics.

4. Third-Party Access:

Django-Chronics does not provide access to user data to any third-party entities.

5. Data Security:

Django-Chronics implements security measures to safeguard user information. The platform is hosted on Heroku, utilizes AWS RDS for PostgreSQL, and securely stores media files in an S3 bucket

6. Cookies and Tracking:

Django-Chronics does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy:

Users can check this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Information:

For privacy-related concerns or questions, users can contact Django-Chronics at: astanindenys@outlook.com
